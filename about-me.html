Hi, I'm Yuri!

Originally from Brazil, I’m currently living and studying UX Design in The Hague, Netherlands.

I have a background in customer service, where I developed empathy and communication skills—two things I now bring into my design process.

I enjoy digital drawing, experimenting with color, and giving visual form to ideas. I'm also someone who finds beauty in organized chaos and expressive layouts.

I believe design should be intuitive, inclusive, and crafted with care.

When I’m not studying or prototyping, you’ll likely find me sketching, watching horror movies, or discovering new spots around the city.
